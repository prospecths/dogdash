About Us

        Imagine a group of friends, all students at Prospect High School, who share a love for wagging tails and furry cuddles. That's us! We've formed a special club dedicated to making a difference, not just in our school, but in the lives of animals who need our help.
        We're not just any club; we're a team of dedicated dog walkers, and we're on a mission! Every step we take, every happy pup we walk, brings us closer to our goal: to raise $1,000 for The Humane Society. This amazing organization works tirelessly to provide shelter, food, and care to animals who have been abandoned, lost, or simply need a helping hand.
        We believe that even small actions can create big changes. By combining our passion for dogs with our desire to give back, we're hoping to make a real impact on the lives of animals in our community.
        We're working hard, organizing walks, and spreading the word about our cause. We want to show everyone that high school students can make a difference. Every dollar we raise will go directly to The Humane Society, helping them continue their vital work of caring for animals in need.
